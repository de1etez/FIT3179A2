Beijing 2022 Olympic Winter Games

The Winter Olympic Games, also known as the Winter Olympics and the Winter Olympics, are the Olympic Games for winter sports, held every four years, with the main feature of the event being winter sports held on ice and snow, such as ice skating and skiing, which are suitable for winter. The XXIV Olympic Winter Games, also known as the 2022 Winter Olympics, take place in Beijing, the capital of the People's Republic of China, from February 4 to February 20, 2022.
