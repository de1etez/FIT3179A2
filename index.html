Beijing 2022 Olympic Winter Games

The Winter Olympic Games, also known as the Winter Olympics and the Winter Olympics, are the Olympic Games for winter sports, held every four years, with the main feature of the event being winter sports held on ice and snow, such as ice skating and skiing, which are suitable for winter. The XXIV Olympic Winter Games, also known as the 2022 Winter Olympics, take place in Beijing, the capital of the People's Republic of China, from February 4 to February 20, 2022. The data shows, the distribution of athletes from different regions, the distribution of sports and the ratio of men to women in the sports and the ranking of national teams in terms of the number of medals.

Summary

Throughout the Winter Olympics, athletes are not evenly distributed, mainly in developed countries. Because of the large number of participants from developed countries, most of the medals were won by developed countries. Although developing countries like China won 9 gold medals, the overall number is still behind the developed countries, and perhaps more resources need to be invested in the athletes to get better results.
